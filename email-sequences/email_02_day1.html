The #1 Rule Experienced Players Never Break 🀄

Quick tip for your next game — this one saves more games than you'd think:

Never pass jokers during the Charleston.

Jokers are the most flexible tiles on the table. Passing one hands your opponent a weapon they can use immediately. Hold onto them until you're fully committed to a hand — and even then, think twice.

Sounds obvious. But I see players do it every session, usually because they haven't committed to a direction yet.

The fix: Decide on your hand direction by tile 3 or 4. Once you have direction, jokers become precious. Never pass precious things.
