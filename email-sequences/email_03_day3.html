The 3-Hand Selection Framework 🀄

Most players stare at their tiles and pick whatever looks "prettiest." That's a trap. Here's the framework that works:

Choose your hand in 3 steps (30 seconds max):

1. Count suits. 1 suit = stay focused. 3 suits = start eliminating immediately.
2. Spot pairs and runs. Pairs = flexibility. A near-run in one suit = chase that direction.
3. Match the card. Find ONE hand on the NMJL card that matches what you already have — not what you wish you had. Build toward that.

Commit. Don't second-guess after round 5. Players who choose fast and stick to it win consistently. Players who stay "flexible" too long usually end with nothing.
